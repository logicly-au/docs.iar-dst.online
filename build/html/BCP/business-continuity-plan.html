1. Business Continuity Plan¶

1.1. Identified Risk: Facility loss - Datacom Hosting Facility¶

Strategic Data client facing services (applications) are hosted at the Datacom shared hosting facility.

1.1.1. Short term: one week or less¶

If the event is assessed to be resolved in one (1) week or less Strategic Data will:

  • inform all clients affected by the event,
  • maintain active communication on the status of the event with all clients affected by the event,
  • re-assess the event daily in order to identify if the severity / duration is greater than expected.

Recovery will not be attempted until it has been determined that the event will last more than one week.

1.1.2. Long term: More than one week¶

Strategic Data will notify all clients and stakeholders of the event and initiate disaster recovery processes as appropriate; including, retrieval of backups from IronMountain and service re-establishment as required.

1.2. Identified Risk: Facility loss - Fitzroy Office¶

Strategic Data’s development and client support infrastructure is largely hosted in the server room at the Fitzroy office.

1.2.1. Short term¶

Management will assess to what extent it makes sense for staff to work from an alternate location - including from home.

1.2.2. Long term¶

As per the process for a long term event at the hosting facility.

  1. Reassign Project tickets to the 2IC and assign a new 2IC.
